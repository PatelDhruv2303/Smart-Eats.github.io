Chicken Cobb Salad

Prep: 15 min Cook: 20 min Total: 35 min Serves: 2
20g Protein
15g Fat
10g Carbs
320 Calories

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill until fully cooked, about 6-7 minutes per side. Let cool, then slice.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, arrange the romaine lettuce as the base.
  4. Arrange sliced chicken, hard-boiled eggs, avocado, cherry tomatoes, blue cheese, bacon, and red onion in rows on top of the lettuce.
  5. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving.
  6. Toss gently and serve immediately.
